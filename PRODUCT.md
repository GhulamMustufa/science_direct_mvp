# Product

Research publishing and discovery platform inspired by ScienceDirect.

## Users

- Readers
- Authors
- Editors
- Admins

## Features

### Public
- Home page
- Search
- Journal listing
- Article details
- PDF viewing

### Author
- Dashboard
- Publications
- Submission tracking

### Admin
- Users
- Journals
- Articles
- Analytics

## Publishing

Our platform handles the end-to-end publishing workflow:

- Author Submission (PDF manuscripts)
- Desk Review by Admin/Editor
- Revision cycles (if requested)
- Acceptance & Publication
- Tagging to Volumes
- Discovery & Search
- Profiles & Analytics

## Out of Scope

- AI recommendations
- Citation graph
- Institutional licensing
- Elasticsearch
- Multi-tenancy
